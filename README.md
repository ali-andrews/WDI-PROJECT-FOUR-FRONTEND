# WDI-PROJECT-FOUR

<p>The Baby Bag is an end-to-end service that enables you to order all of your necessary weekly baby supplies in one easy to use, online location and receive these direct to your home or office. We deliver baby food, formula, nappies and baby care products to busy little families each week on a ‘fast as possible’ basis, committing to same day delivery for orders that arrive through our website before midday.<br><br>

The Baby Bag was born with busy mums and dads in mind and is designed to provide you with flexibility to allow your shopping experience to suit you.</p>
